Hans Oil and Gas Industry

Hans subsector includes the production of crude petroleum, the mining and extraction of oil from oil shale and oil sands, and the production of natural gas, sulfur recovery from natural gas, and the recovery of hydrocarbon liquids. The growth of the world economy and population is driving the global demand for Energy. To meet this demand, Hans delivers ground-breaking projects in a challenging offshore market. Our offshore oil and gas activities provide integrated solutions with a focus on the installation, stabilisation, and protection of offshore oil and gas infrastructure. Hans has led the market in subsea rock installation for than thirty years. We has managed to maintain that position for continously challenging ourselves and improving our expertise.
